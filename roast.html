The Best Pot Roast Ever

Description

This isn't just any pot roast—it's The Best Pot Roast Ever, and once you taste it, you'll know why. Slow-cooked to tender perfection, this roast is infused with deep, savory flavors from fresh herbs, garlic, and a rich beef broth. The meat practically falls apart with a fork, while hearty carrots, potatoes, and onions soak up all that delicious, meaty goodness. It’s comfort food at its finest, the kind that warms your soul and fills your kitchen with mouthwatering aromas.

Perfect for Sunday dinners, holiday gatherings, or cozy nights in, this pot roast is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. It requires minimal prep but delivers maximum flavor, thanks to the magic of low-and-slow cooking. Serve it with crusty bread or over a bed of buttery mashed potatoes, and watch it disappear fast. This is the kind of recipe that becomes a family tradition—the one everyone asks for by name.

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Beef Stock

Steps

  1. Put Beef Stock into a dutch oven, with all raw ingredients. Pot Roast, Carrots, and Potatoes.
  2. Put Pot Roast into Oven
  3. Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees F
  4. Put Vodka Sauce onto heat.
  5. Put a spoon-full of pasta water into sauce prior to mixing noodles in.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
