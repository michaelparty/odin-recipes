Chicken Pasta alla Vodka

Description

Creamy, dreamy, and downright irresistible, Chicken Alla Vodka takes your pasta night to the next level. This rich, velvety sauce starts with a base of sautéed garlic and shallots, deglazed with a splash of vodka to bring out deep, smooth flavors. Crushed tomatoes simmer into the mix, followed by a swirl of heavy cream that transforms everything into a luscious pink sauce. Toss in tender, juicy pieces of pan-seared chicken, and you’ve got a dish that hits every savory note.

Served over your favorite pasta—penne, rigatoni, or even fettuccine—Chicken Alla Vodka is both elegant and comforting. It’s the perfect balance of bold and creamy, with just enough kick to keep it exciting. Whether you're cooking for a date night or craving a restaurant-quality dinner at home, this recipe delivers serious flavor without complicated steps. Add fresh basil and a sprinkle of parmesan on top, and it’s officially a showstopper.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Alla Vodka Sauce
  • Chicken
  • Parmasean

Steps

  1. Put on water to a boil for Penne Noodles
  2. Prep egg wash and breading for Chicken.
  3. Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees F
  4. Put Vodka Sauce onto heat.
  5. Put a spoon-full of pasta water into sauce prior to mixing noodles in.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
