Bangin' Lasagna

Description

Get ready to impress your taste buds with this Bangin' Lasagna—a bold, flavor-packed twist on a classic comfort dish. Layers of rich, meaty sauce made with seasoned ground beef and Italian sausage are stacked between perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, creamy ricotta blended with herbs, and a generous helping of gooey mozzarella and parmesan. Every bite is a balance of savory, cheesy goodness with just the right amount of garlic and spice to keep things interesting.

This lasagna doesn’t just feed the crowd—it wins them over. Whether you’re cooking for family dinner, a potluck, or just meal-prepping for the week, Bangin' Lasagna brings the heat and heartiness. It’s a no-fuss recipe with big results, and it only gets better the next day. Serve it with garlic bread and a fresh salad, and you’ve got yourself a certified crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Bangin' Red Sauce
  • Cheesiest Cheese

Steps

  1. Layer ingredients in this order: Pasta, Red Sauce, then Cheesiest Cheese.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 450 Degrees F.
  3. Put Dish into oven and let cook for 20 minuets.
  4. Take out and let cool for 5 minuets before garnish.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
